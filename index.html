ECE286 Survey

This is an data collection form for our course project for ECE286: Probability and Statistics. In this project, we are investigating how the presence of pop-up ads impacts user response time and accuracy. You will be given 2 different resumes with different contents, and asked to answer a series of 5 multiple choice questions each. These questions themselves are not difficult, and they simply require you to identify and comprehend certain areas of the texts themselves. One resume will have pop up ads, while the other will not. We will collect only response accuracy and response time data, no more no less. All other information is for identity verification purposes (we just want to make sure you are actually eligible for the study). If you find any issues, please do not hesitate to reach out at ry.fang@mail.utoronto.ca or samueldy.ho@mail.utoronto.ca. Thank you for participating in our study! We really appreciate it.
